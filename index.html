Bot Commands

Here are some bot commands

  • Play a song .

    Play a song either from a URL or select from a list of returned results.
    Aliases: -p
    Bot Permissions: Connect , Speak , Embed Links
    Example: -play Despacito

  • Check What is Playing.

    Show the currently playing song in an embed listing the progress, the requester, the author and the progress bar.
    Aliases: -nowplaying , -np
    Bot Permissions: Embed Links

  • Queue System - Shows all currently enqueued songs

    List every song that's waiting to be played along with the requester of that song.
    Aliases: -queue, -q
    Bot Permissions: Embed Links

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions

  • How do I add Cord DJ to my server ?

    To add Cord DJ, simply click on the Invite button on the top right of this page, then select a server that Cord DJ will be added to.

  • Does Cord DJ support other languages ?

    Unfortunately, Cord DJ only supports English. Depending on bot growth, our team will try adding support for more languages.

  • Will Cord DJ always be free ?

    Yes, Cord DJ is and will always be 100% free to use. However, if you want to support our project, feel free to donate, this helps a ton.
